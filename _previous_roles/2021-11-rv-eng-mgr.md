I managed the central Video engineering team of 6 back-end engineers in the US and UK, with additional contractors from South America. We built a new SaaS video ecosystem to support up to two billion unique video-on-demand (VOD) play sessions per year. We also handled live-streaming, captioning, digital asset management, ads integration, analytics, and distribution. We onboarded [CNET](https://www.cnet.com/), [ZDNet](https://www.zdnet.com/), [GameSpot](https://www.gamespot.com/), [GiantBomb](https://www.giantbomb.com/), [TV Guide](https://www.tvguide.com/), [OnlineMBA](https://www.onlinemba.com/), [Cord Cutters News](https://www.cordcuttersnews.com/), [BestColleges](https://www.bestcolleges.com/), [NurseJournal](https://www.nursejournal.com/) and were in the process of bringing [Healthline](https://www.healthline.com/), [Metacritic](https://www.metacritic.com/) and [LonelyPlanet](https://www.lonelyplanet.com/) onto our platform. We worked with vendors such as [AWS Elemental](https://www.elemental.com/), [Fastly](https://www.fastly.com/), [Bitmovin](https://bitmovin.com/), [Rev](https://www.rev.com/), and [Mux](https://www.mux.com/). The engineers built API orchestration and microservices in [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/) ([Express](https://expressjs.com/)) & [Go/Golang](https://go.dev/) ([Goa](https://goa.design/)), CMS integrations in [PHP](https://www.php.net/) ([Symfony](https://symfony.com/), [WordPress](https://wordpress.com/)), and deployed into [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/) and [GCP](https://cloud.google.com/) with [CircleCI](https://circleci.com/) on infrastructure managed with [Terraform](https://www.terraform.io/).
