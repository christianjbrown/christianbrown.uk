* Public-facing stewarding and check-in, managing booking queries, and assisting patients experiencing complex needs such as medical exemptions, differing vaccination requirements, eligibility disputes, early second-dose requests; pregnant, the elderly, and children; as well as those affected by needle phobia, extreme anxiety, or other mental and physical health issues.
