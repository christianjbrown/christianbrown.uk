I volunteered to support NHS’s COVID-19 vaccination programme (420hrs over 70 six-hour shifts), primarily at the Mass Vaccination Centre at the Science Museum in London. I managed public-facing stewarding, check-in, and booking queries, supporting patients with complex medical, eligibility, and mental health needs. I assisted diverse populations ranging from undocumented migrants to VIPs, including diplomats and public figures. I liaised with museum security and police to handle incidents involving protests, uninvited media, and anti-vaccine activity.
