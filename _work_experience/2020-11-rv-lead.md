* I led the architecture and served as the lead back-end engineer for the development of an interim replacement video-on-demand (VOD) solution, designed to support up to a billion video play sessions per year. This system was built to handle the anticipated traffic from the brands Red Ventures acquired from ViacomCBS.
* I was also on the working group managing the overall migration, collaborating with the product team on vendor evaluations, setting transition targets in line with the Transition Services Agreement (TSA), ensuring engineering deliverables stayed on track, and aligning stakeholder expectations across both organisations.
* By eliminating a content management vendor, I achieved cost savings of US$430k in the first year and US$275k annually thereafter.
