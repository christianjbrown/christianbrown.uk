I manage a team of 6-7 'full stack' engineers in the global shipping department, based in London with staff in Toronto and contractors in Cyprus. We are responsible for revenue-sensitive post-sales shipping experience for sellers on the web. We use carrier integrators to purchase shipping from couriers and postal services in the US, CA, UK, DE, and AU markets. We facilitate a range workflows such as pay-on-print, pay-on-scan (QR code), bulk shipping, returns, tracking, cross-border exports, and ship to [eBay Vault](https://www.ebay.com/vault) or [eBay Authentication Guarantee centres](https://www.ebay.com/help/selling/selling-tools/ebay-authenticity-guarantee?id=4644). The engineers build microservices in [React](https://reactjs.org/), [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/), [Marko](https://markojs.com/), and [Java](https://www.java.com/).

I have transformed the team from project-feature to product-ownership driven, introduced velocity, reduced production issues & customer reported defects by over half, and empowered engineers to think about architecture and maintainability over short term results. I also handle long-term planning / project management, staff training, performance management, attrition, hiring, and promotions.
