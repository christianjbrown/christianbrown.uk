I manage a team of full-stack engineers in eBay's global shipping department, based in London, Toronto, and Cyprus. My team are responsible for revenue-sensitive post-sales shipping experience for sellers on the web in US, CA, UK, DE, and AU markets. We handle over 25 million label purchases per month, providing solutions like pay-on-print, pay-on-scan, bulk shipping, returns, tracking, insurance/claims, [cross-border exports](https://www.ebay.co.uk/sellercentre/global-sales/how-to-export){:target="_blank"}{:title="Cross-Border Exports on eBay"}, and ship-to [eBay Vault](https://www.ebay.com/vault){:target="_blank"}{:title="eBay Vault"} or [eBay Authentication Guarantee centres](https://www.ebay.co.uk/help/selling/selling-tools/ebay-authenticity-guarantee?id=4644){:target="_blank"}{:title="eBay Authentication Guarantee centres"}. The engineers build Single Page Applications in [React](https://reactjs.org/){:target="_blank"}{:title="React"}, supported by back-end microservices built in [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/){:target="_blank"}{:title="Node.js"}.

My responsibilities include: overseeing technical decisions and architecture, capacity planning, project management, stakeholder engagement, performance calibration, career development and hiring. Under my leadership, we have transitioned from a project-based to a product-centric, ownership-driven model, leading to a drastic reduction in production issues and customer-reported defects. I empower engineers to prioritise long-term architecture and maintainability over short term results.
