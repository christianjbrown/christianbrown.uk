I manage a team of 6-7 'full stack' engineers in the global shipping department, based in London with staff in Toronto and contractors in Cyprus. We are responsible for post-sales experience for sellers on the web, including revenue-sensitive shipping/postage purchasing and label generation systems. We use carrier integrators such as [Pitney Bowes](https://www.pitneybowes.com/), [Scurri](https://www.scurri.com/), [EasyShip](https://www.easyship.com/), [EasyPost](https://www.easypost.com/) & [Packlink](https://www.packlink.com/), shipping via [USPS](https://www.usps.com/), [UPS](https://www.ups.com/), [FedEx](https://www.fedex.com/), [Royal Mail](https://www.royalmail.com/), [Parcelforce](https://www.parcelforce.com/), [Hermes (DE)](https://www.myhermes.de/), [Deutsche Post](https://www.deutschepost.de/), [DHL](https://www.dhl.com/), [Canada Post](https://www.canadapost.ca/), [Australia Post](https://auspost.com.au/), [Hubbed](https://hubbed.com/), [DPD](https://www.dpd.com/), [Yodel](https://www.yodel.co.uk/), and [Evri (UK)](https://www.evri.com/). We operate these services in the US, CA, UK, DE, and AU, facilitating workflows such as pay-on-print, pay-on-scan (QR code), bulk shipping, returns, cross-border exports, and ship to [eBay Vault](https://www.ebay.com/vault) or [eBay Authentication Guarantee centres](https://www.ebay.com/help/selling/selling-tools/ebay-authenticity-guarantee?id=4644).

Since starting, I have transformed the team from project-feature to product-ownership driven, increased productivity & introduced velocity measurement, reduced production issues & customer reported defects by over half, and empowered engineers to think about architecture and maintainability over short term results. I also handle long-term planning / project management, staff training, performance management, redundancies, hiring, and promotions. 

The engineers build microservices in [React](https://reactjs.org/), [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/), [Marko](https://markojs.com/), and [Java](https://www.java.com/), deployed into eBay's private cloud infrastructure using proprietary or extended open-source tools.
