I manage a team of 6-7 'full stack' engineers in the global shipping department, based in London with some staff in Toronto and contractors in Cyprus. We are responsible for post-sales shipping experience for sellers on the web, including revenue-sensitive shipping/postage purchasing and label generation systems. We integrate into carrier integrators such as Pitney Bowes, Scurri, EasyShip, EasyPost & Packlink, shipping via postal services & carriers such as USPS, UPS, FedEx, Royal Mail, Parcelforce, Hermes (DE), Deutsche Post, DHL, Canada Post, Australia Post, Hubbed, DPD, Yodel, and Evri (UK). We operate these services in the US, CA, UK, DE, and AU, facilitating workflows such as pay-on-print, pay-on-scan (QR code), bulk shipping, returns, cross-border exports, and ship to eBay Vault or eBay Authentication Guarantee centres.

Since starting I have transformed the team from project-feature to product-ownership driven, increased productivity & introduced velocity measurement, reduced production issues & customer reported defects by over half, and empowered engineers to think about architecture and maintainability over short term results. I also handle long-term planning / project management, staff training, performance management, redundancies, hiring, and promotions. 

The engineers build microservices in React, Node.js, Marko, and Java, deployed into eBay's private cloud infrastructure using proprietary or extended open-source tools.
