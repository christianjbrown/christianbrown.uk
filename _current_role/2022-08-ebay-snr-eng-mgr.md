* I manage a front-end team of full-stack engineers in eBay's global shipping department, based in London, Toronto, and Cyprus. We are responsible for revenue-sensitive post-sales shipping experience for sellers on the web in US, CA, UK, DE, and AU markets.

* We handle over 20 million label purchases monthly, providing solutions like pay-on-print, pay-on-scan, bulk shipping, returns, tracking, insurance/claims, [cross-border exports](https://www.ebay.co.uk/sellercentre/global-sales/how-to-export){:target="_blank"}, and ship-to [eBay Vault](https://www.ebay.com/vault){:target="_blank"} or [eBay Authentication Guarantee centres](https://www.ebay.co.uk/help/selling/selling-tools/ebay-authenticity-guarantee?id=4644){:target="_blank"}. The engineers build Single Page Applications in [React](https://reactjs.org/){:target="_blank"}, supported by back-end microservices built in [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/){:target="_blank"}.

* Under my leadership, we have transitioned from a project-based to a product-centric, ownership-driven model, leading to a more than 50% reduction in production issues and customer-reported defects. I empower engineers to prioritise long-term architecture and maintainability over short term results.

* My responsibilities include six-month planning, project management, stakeholder engagement, staff development, performance calibration, and overseeing hiring and career advancement.
