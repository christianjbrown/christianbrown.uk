We build Single&nbsp;Page&nbsp;Applications in [React](https://reactjs.org/){:target="_blank"}{:title="React"}, complemented by back‑end microservices in [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/){:target="_blank"}{:title="Node.js"}.

My responsibilities include: fostering&nbsp;good&nbsp;engineering, project&nbsp;prioritisation, capacity&nbsp;planning, stakeholder&nbsp;engagement, career&nbsp;development, performance&nbsp;calibration, and recruitment.

I empower engineers to be customer‑focused and consider platform architecture and maintainability over short‑term results, resulting in a significant decrease in production outages and customer‑reported issues.
